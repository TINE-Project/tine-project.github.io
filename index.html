About the Group

A multidisciplinary team pushing the boundaries of how machines understand and generate human language.

We are a research group based in Manchester, conducting cutting-edge work in natural language processing and computational linguistics. Our research emphasizes real-world impact across healthcare, finance, social media, and education.

Our team brings together academics, researchers, and PhD students committed to open science, responsible AI, and inclusive research that benefits society.

Research Themes

We combine foundational NLP research with high-impact, real-world applications.

🧠

Large Language Models

Evaluation, adaptation, and deployment of large language models, including prompting strategies, domain adaptation, and robust benchmark design.

�

Text Simplification & Accessibility

Making text accessible to diverse audiences through simplification, paraphrase generation, and lexical complexity assessment for readers with varying comprehension levels.

🔎

Information Extraction

Turning unstructured text into structured knowledge through named entity recognition, relation extraction, event detection, and practical knowledge graph pipelines.

🌐

Multilingual NLP

Cross-lingual transfer and low-resource modelling, with a focus on fair performance across languages and robust methods for under-resourced settings.

🩺

Biomedical & Clinical NLP

Clinical and biomedical text mining for decision support, evidence synthesis, and scalable analysis of healthcare language data.

⚠️

Misinformation Detection & Fact-Checking

Identifying false claims, detecting misinformation, and verifying facts across social media, news, and user-generated content using NLP and machine learning.

🔍

Question Answering & Retrieval

Retrieval-augmented generation, evidence-grounded QA, and fact verification methods for reliable domain-specific and open-domain systems.

🛡️

Responsible, Ethical & Secure AI

Responsible NLP covering bias analysis, annotation quality, uncertainty quantification, explainability, AI safety, LLM security, and trustworthy AI deployment in enterprise settings.
