Autotrader: Collaboration in Applied NLP & LLMs Research

A multi-year MMU and Autotrader collaboration focused on practical NLP and LLM solutions, from information extraction and sentiment analysis to open-source semantic search.

Back to Projects See Research Themes

Overview

MMU and Autotrader have engaged in a multi-year collaboration exploring a range of NLP projects. This includes information extraction tasks, such as extracting entity spans from automotive advertisements (for example, vehicle condition), alongside aspect-based sentiment analysis and speech-based sentiment analysis.

The current focus of the collaboration is improving semantic search using open-source solutions.

Funding and duration

  • First funding: Feb 2024 to Aug 2025
  • Second funding: Sep 2025 to Feb 2027

Objectives

  • Develop information extraction solutions tailored for the automotive domain
  • Explore efficient NLP approaches for semantic parsing
  • Benchmark different LLMs

Status
